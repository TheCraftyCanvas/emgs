History

Eureka Metal & Glass was created in the early fifties as Eureka Glass Works, Inc., a commercial glass installation sub-contracting firm. In the sixties, Eureka started fabricating and installing aluminum frames and doors. Throughout the seventies, Eureka continued to establish its repertoire throughout Philadelphia and much of the Delaware Valley. Then in 1980, the management torch was passed down the family lines and Eureka saw continued growth and prosperity. In 1995 Eureka purchased the assets of Glass Services Unlimited and started performing service work on aluminum doors and temporary boarding and replacement of damaged glass or aluminum.

Today, Eureka Metal & Glass Services is over 50 years old servicing General Contractors, Owners, and property managers throughout the southeastern PA, NJ, Delaware, New York City, Long Island, New York's Westchester & Rockland Counties, and Southern Connecticut.
