PRODUCTS Below is just a brief list of the many products we can furnish and install...

Common
Product Lines


  • Storefronts

    We furnish & install several manufacturer's standard storefronts in typical anodized finishes, custom paints, and custom cladding. Storefronts can be utilized in several applications, with standard parts and finishes available for installation within 5-6 weeks*
  • Curtainwall

    In addition to storefront, we also furnish & install several manufacturer's standard curtainwalls in typical anodized finishes and custom paints, or with custom cladding. Curtain walls differ from storefront systems in that they are designed to span multiple floors, and take into consideration design requirements such as: thermal expansion and contraction; building sway and movement; water diversion; and thermal efficiency for cost-effective heating, cooling, and lighting in the building. Standard parts and finishes are usually available for installation within 6-10 weeks*
  • Structural Glazing

    Besides standard glazed openings, Eureka can also furnish & install custom structural glazing ranging from exterior butt glazed systems to stacked glass, utilizing dot supports, spider fittings, and/or structural glass fins. Most of our suppliers can furnish glass as large as 60 square feet (up to 8 feet along one dimension). Larger glass lites are available but have a much longer lead time from the fabricators.

  • Skylighting Systems
    • Glass, polycarbonate, and acrylic skylights
    • Fixed & Ventilating
    • Sloped Glazing
    • Vaulted, Domed, Pyramid, Polygon, and Flat Skylights
    • Custom Metal Framed Skylights
    • Installation experience with Kalwall, Naturalite, Solar Innovations, Supersky, and more!
  • Butt Glazed Partitions

    Butt glazed partitions utilizing 1/4", 3/8", 1/2", and 3/4" glass are also available. Glass can be set in your wooden or hollow metal frames, or we can furnish top & bottom aluminum channels.
  • Specialty Glass & Acrylics
    • Decorative Art Glass (Bendheim, McGrory, Rudy Art Glass)
    • Acrylic Panels (3-Form, Fry Reglet)
    • Fire-Rated Glass (Wire Glass, TGP)
    • Laminated Glass (Vanceva)
    • Photo/Art Embedded Glass
    • Filmed Glass
    • Lead-Lined Glass
    • Integral Blind Units (Unicel Architectural, Kyler Industries)
  • All Glass Entrances

    Transom & Transomless Clear Tempered Frameless Glass Doors are configurable with:

    • Top & Bottom Patch Fittings
    • Top & Bottom 3-3/8", 4", & 6" Rails
    • Concealed Overhead Closers or Floor Closers
    • Concealed Overhead Maglocks or Surface Applied Maglocks**
    • Custom Push/Pulls and Pull/Pulls
    • Structural Glass Fins
  • Custom Glass Entrances

    In addition to standard glass entrances, Eureka is experienced in assisting with taking custom glass entrances from conceptual design through engineering to final production and installation. Previous projects included:

    • Custom storefront doors up to 10' tall
    • Custom shaped glass doors
    • Top hung, trackless all-glass sliders
    • Roller & track assembly glass sliders
  • Aluminum Swing Entrances

    • Narrow, medium, and wide stile aluminum swing doors for 1/4" glass and 1" glass units
    • Impact resistant aluminum swing doors
  • Sliding Entrances

    • Aluminum framed sliders
    • Showroom sliders
    • Automatic sliders
  • Glass Stair & Guard Rails

    • Glass Railings with Offset Handrails
    • Glass Railings with Cap Rails
    • Glass Railings with Stainless Steel Cladding for stringers & slab edge supports
  • Stainless Steel Paneling
    • Wall Paneling
    • Column Covers
    • Stair Stringers
    • Convector Covers
    • Signbands
  • Miscellaneous

    • Glass Canopies

    • Glass Tabletop Inserts
    • Custom Mirrors
    • Glass Display Cases & Shelf Assemblies
    • Aluminum Muntins
    • Aluminum Sunshades
