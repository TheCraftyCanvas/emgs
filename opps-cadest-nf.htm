CAD Operator/Estimator

We are seeking individuals to assist our design staff. The successful candidate will be a highly motivated and energetic performer that can take concepts from notes and document them in technical plans/blueprints. Further, they must exhibit strong organizational skills, professional standards, and be capable of maintaining a positive attitude in the face of constantly changing priorities. A strong desire to learn will be welcomed by a friendly organization with the willingness to train the right individual in the specifics of our company.

Key Responsibilities:

* Editing existing plans and drawings using AutoCAD LT 2005.
* Creating and maintaining plans and drawings from architect's renditions and sketches.
* Assisting in writing bids and estimating costs for new projects.
* Researching new architectural products for possible use/integration into projects.

Skills Required:

* Experience using AutoCAD products for 2-D design work.
* Familiar with Microsoft Word & Excel.
* Good communication and organization skills.
* Attention to detail and strong mathematical skills.
* Familiarity in estimating helpful, but not necessary.
* Familiarity in fabrication, installation, or design of architectural glass or metal structures helpful, but not necessary.

This is a full-time position based in our Northeast Philadelphia facility. Telecommuting and contract work is not available.
